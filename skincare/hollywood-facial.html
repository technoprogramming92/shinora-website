What Is Hollywood Facial?

Treatment Details

How it's done?
The Hollywood Facial is a 1 hour advanced therapy that includes the use of a carbon mask and a Q-switched laser treatment.
Benefits:
Glowing: Radiance, Rejuvenation, Hydration

Description

Hollywood facial is a type of facial that uses the carbon mask and gently exfoliates your skin, while also providing important vitamins and antioxidants. It is actually an advanced laser procedure.

The laser removes abnormal pigmentation, which is broken down into fragments that your body can absorb. This makes your skin appear more even-toned and bright.

The laser also burns the underlying tissues, which plumps the skin and stimulates collagen remodeling. The procedure concludes with a medical-grade facial, giving you the 'no make-up' confidence we all want.

How to Prepare for Hollywood Facial?

Before your therapy, refrain from using retinoids like Retin-A, Vitamin C, and glycolic acids for five days.

Additionally, avoid skin treatments such as microdermabrasion, professional or home exfoliation, Botox, and fillers for at least two weeks.

These precautions help ensure optimal results by minimizing potential interference with your treatment.

What to Expect During Hollywood Facial Therapy?

The Hollywood Facial, which lasts 60 minutes, combines cutting-edge procedures to revitalize your skin. A nutritious carbon mask rich with essential vitamins and antioxidants is first applied. This mask not only detoxifies but also gently exfoliates the skin, priming it for the next treatment. Following that, a Q-switched laser is carefully guided over the carbon mask to target troublesome pigmentation.

The Q-Switched laser successfully converts pigmentation problems into absorbable fragments, resulting in a more uniform and radiant skin. The laser enhances the body's natural absorption mechanism by fragmenting melanin, resulting in cleaner and brighter skin. This novel method improves skin tone by correcting unevenness and discolouration, resulting in a more youthful appearance.

Furthermore, the Q-Switched laser delivers precise heat to the underlying tissues, causing collagen remodeling. This warm stimulation not only plumps the skin, but also stimulates the formation of new collagen fibers. As a result, the skin appears firmer, smoother, and younger, with restored vigor and radiance.
