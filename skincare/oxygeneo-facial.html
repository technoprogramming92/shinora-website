What Is Oxygeneo Facial?

Treatment Details

How it's done?
We breathe because our bodies require oxygen to function properly. The idea behind oxygeneo facials is that bright, healthy skin results from properly oxygenated skin cells.
Benefits:
Oxygenation, Exfoliation, Nourishment

Description

Since no chemicals or injections are used, an Oxygeneo facial is regarded as a "nonmedical" technique.

With the help of the body's inherent ability to oxygenate the skin from the inside out, the OxyGeneo facial elevates skincare to a whole new level. In contrast to standard oxygen facials, OxyGeneo uses the Bohr effect, a three-in-one method, to increase skin oxygenation.

In contrast to standard oxygen facials, OxyGeneo uses the Bohr effect, a three-in-one method, to increase skin oxygenation. followed by steps Exfoliation, CO2-Rich Environment and Bohr Effect, Infusion of Targeted Serums.

This helps to improve the functionality of the skin and slows down the aging process, which improves the appearance of the skin.

How to Prepare for Oxygeneo Facial?

Prior to an OxyGeneo facial, there aren't any significant preparation measures that must be completed. Still, if you want to expedite the procedure, you should go for appointment without wearing any makeup.

What to Expect During Oxygeneo Facial Therapy?

A customized handheld wand and treatment gel are used for exfoliating in the first step of the OxyGeneo facial. To do a light microdermabrasion, this method involves gliding the wand over the skin. This mechanical exfoliation leaves the skin feeling smoother and more luminous by efficiently clearing clogged pores and eliminating dead skin cells. It also sets the skin up for further treatment procedures.

When the OxyGeneo facial is performed after exfoliation, it creates an environment rich in CO2, which causes the Bohr effect. Applying the customized therapy gel with the portable wand causes the body's natural response to improve oxygen transport to the tissues by stimulating elevated CO2 levels. Increased oxygenation and circulation at the skin's surface are brought about by this oxygen infusion, which results in a complexion that is more vibrant and renewed.

Targeted serums are infused into the oxygen-rich skin as the last step of the OxyGeneo facial. Strong active ingredients designed to target particular skin issues like anti-aging, brightening, or hydration are present in these serums. The Bohr effect produces an oxygen-rich environment that makes it easier for these serums to penetrate deeply into the skin, increasing their effectiveness. By ensuring that the skin receives a concentrated amount of advantageous nutrients, this infusion stage promotes the general health and vitality of the skin.
