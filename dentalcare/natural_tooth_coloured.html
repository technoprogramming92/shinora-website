What Is Natural Tooth Coloured treatment?

Treatment Details

How it's done?
Teeth that have been broken, decaying, or discolored can be repaired using natural tooth-colored restorations. restorations composed of porcelain, ceramics, and composite materials, which have qualities similar to those of natural teeth.
Benefits:
Whitening, Aesthetics, Confidence

Description

Tooth discoloration refers to changes in the color of your teeth, which can manifest as specks of black or white, yellowing, or a loss of brilliance.

This condition can be caused by various factors such as tobacco use, tooth damage, poor oral hygiene, and consumption of certain foods, beverages, or medications.

Natural tooth coloring treatments are commonly employed to address discoloration, restoring the teeth to their natural shade.

Additionally, this treatment utilize tooth-colored restorations to repair damaged, decayed, or discolored teeth, enhancing their appearance and function.
