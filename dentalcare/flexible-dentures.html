What Is Flexible Dentures?

Treatment Details

How it's done?
Flexible dentures are created using a flexible resin material that allows them to bend and conform to the shape of the patient's mouth. This material is injected into a mold of the patient's oral cavity, ensuring a custom fit for enhanced comfort and functionality.
Benefits:
Comfort, Flexibility, Adaptability

Description

Flexible partial dentures offer a modern alternative to traditional dentures, featuring a thin thermoplastic material that bends and adapts seamlessly to the shape of your mouth, enhancing comfort and functionality.

Unlike conventional dentures, flexible partial dentures are designed specifically for individuals with some remaining natural teeth, providing a softer and more flexible solution that addresses common issues associated with wearing dentures.

With flexible partial dentures, patients can enjoy improved comfort and ease of wear, thanks to the innovative use of a flexible resin material that ensures a customized fit and eliminates many of the discomforts often experienced with traditional dentures.
