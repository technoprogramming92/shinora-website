What Is Venners / Laminates?

Treatment Details

How it's done?
Dental veneers are custom-made porcelain ‘shells’ designed to cover the front of the tooth to improve the tooth color and also protect the teeth from cavity.
Benefits:
Brighter smile, enhances appearance, speedy results, long-lasting effects.

Description

The front surfaces of your teeth are covered by veneers. To make them, technicians employ premium dental materials such as porcelain or composite that is tinted like teeth.

Veneers come in a variety of forms, including as composite, porcelain, no-prep, and removable varieties whic are effective solution to hide stains, chips, cracks, and other flaws in appearance.

One of the most popular procedures in cosmetic dentistry is veneer placement. Various veneer varieties are offered based on your own objectives.

How to Prepare for Veneers / Laminates?

Before the Veneer Treatment, a routine dental check-up and cleaning is done . This ensures that the teeth are free of plaque and tartar, providing a clean surface for the laminater to work on.

Be sure to use a soft-bristled toothbrush and non-abrasive fluoride toothpaste. Avoid whitening products, as they can scratch your veneers.

There’s no downtime following veneer placement. People who receive dental veneers can return to normal activities the same day. You can also eat or drink immediately after your appointment, as long as the anesthesia has worn off.

What to Expect During Teeth Whitening treatment?

The entire veneers procedure will take two dentist appointments. A dental veneer is a thin porcelain shell bonded to the front of your tooth. Firstly dentist remove the top layer of enamel from the fronts of your teeth and then take dental impressions.

In second appointment,dentist will make sure that the porcelain veneers are the right shape, size, and color for your teeth.

After that process, they’ll clean, polish, and etch the receiving teeth for the bonding process. This is done so that the veneers bond strongly to the fronts of the teeth.
